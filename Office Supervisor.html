Office Supervisor

In this role I was in charge of a small team that was tasked with running all of the admin and cash office duties for the store. Making sure that many time sensitive jobs were completed correctly and promptly as well as handling all the cashing up and banking that was required each day. Working with multiple bespoke computer systems to handle various elements of this role.

Duties

  • Running payroll weekly, calculating hours for all members of staff
  • Cashing up tills on a day to day basis including banking, change ordering and dealing with any discrepancy issues that would arise
  • Tracking lateness, absence and holiday for the store
  • Responsible for daily reports including negative stock issues, chasing overdue deliveries and store price changes/ sale POS events
  • Working with all staff as a conduit to head office for information requests
  • Management of the store's stock file, amending and investigating where needed.
