Self Employed

Using the knowledge and skills that I learnt over my Bachelors and Masters courses I started working as a full time artist, something that I still carry on with in my spare time. Having had reasonable success in galleries across the UK and most recently being part of Europe’s biggest street art festival I’ve gained a lot of useful knowledge including;

  • Working directly with clients fulfilling specific requirements in commission work.
  • Careful time management having to work to specific deadlines for competition entries and show deadlines.
